Getting Started

Let's build your digital estate plan.

Walk through six steps to document the people, accounts, devices, and documents your family needs to know about.

What you'll set up

Each step takes just a few minutes — and you can always come back to add more later.

Step 1 — Contacts

Family, executors, and caregivers who should have access.

~3 min

Step 2 — Accounts

Email, banking, social media, subscriptions — no passwords needed.

~5 min

Step 3 — Devices

Phones, laptops, tablets — where they live and what to do with each.

~3 min

Step 4 — Estate Docs

Wills, powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and trusts.

~5 min

Step 5 — Documents

Insurance, tax records, property deeds, IDs, and medical records.

~5 min

Step 6 — Family Notes

Personal messages, wishes, and guidance for the people you love.

~5 min

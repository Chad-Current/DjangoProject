Security and Review

We establish a foundation of trust and privacy from the start, ensuring your sensitive data is handled only according to agreed-upon protocols We guarantee a secure hand-off, including setting up a vault that you control completely.

We utilize a proprietary process to prompt for and integrate often-missed accounts such as utility portals, medical accounts, subscriptions, and less-frequently used social media. The result is a Master Account Inventory, compiled into a secure spreadsheet, detailing critical information like usernames, recovery options, and the precise location of credentials.

We introduce a secure vault system and ensure that key passwords are correctly imported and stored. We focus on the most critical step: establishing a strong master password and agreeing on a reliable, specific methodology for "executor access". This ensures that your chosen representative can gain access when needed, without compromising daily security.

We establish the specialized “Family Legacy” folder structure for rapid location of essential categories, such as: Documents (Legal, Financial, Medical) Media (Photos, Videos) Accounts (Logins & Recovery information).

We provide and complete a specialized template designed to cover every necessity for your digital assets. This document precisely maps out access information, including the location of the physical will and the secure vault. We ensure all special wishes are documented, such as instructions for social media memorialization or specific photo sharing requests.

We ensure essential security measures are addressed during the hand-off session. This involves advising on necessary actions like updating software, enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) on major accounts, and securely signing out of unused devices. A critical component is establishing an “Annual Digital Review” calendar reminder to ensure your plan remains current and secure.

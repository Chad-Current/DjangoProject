Security & Privacy

How do I know my information is safe with your service?

Your privacy and security are taken seriously at every step.

  • Mail-only intake: All documents are sent using a prepaid USPS Priority Mail envelope with tracking. No email. No uploads. No online portals.
  • Single-handler process: Your documents are handled by one person only â€” me. There are no assistants, contractors, or third-party scanning services involved.
  • Private handling: Documents are opened and processed in a private, controlled workspace. Nothing is handled in public or shared environments.
  • No originals kept: Clients are instructed to send copies only. If an original is accidentally included, it is not scanned and is returned immediately.

Self-Service Password Reset

  • We use a self-service password reset system, just click on forgot password on the login page and an e-mail will be sent with reset instructions
Who will have access to my passwords and accounts?

The Account Owner

  • Only the account owner will be given access to information. We cannot see any passwords or sensitive information related to your account.

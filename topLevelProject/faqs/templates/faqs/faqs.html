Help & Common Questions

Click any question below to read the answer. If you need more help, scroll to the bottom of the page and we'll get back to you.

Keeping Your Information Safe
Is my personal information safe on this website? Show answer Hide answer

Yes — your information is kept private and secure. Here is how we protect you:

  • Your documents are mailed in using a prepaid envelope — nothing is sent over the internet.
  • Only one person handles your paperwork. No outside companies or extra staff are involved.
  • Please only send copies of your documents, not originals. Any original accidentally sent will be returned to you unopened.
Can anyone else see my passwords? Show answer Hide answer

No. Only you can see your passwords and private account details.

We cannot see them either. The only people who can access your information are the trusted family members or friends that you choose to share specific items with.

What if I forget my password to log in? Show answer Hide answer

No problem — it happens to everyone. On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link. We will send a reset link to your email address.

If you no longer have access to your email address, please contact us directly and we will help you recover your account safely.

Your Family After You're Gone
Who will be able to see my information when I pass away? Show answer Hide answer

Only the people you choose. You decide exactly which family members or trusted friends can see which items — such as a specific bank account, a document, or a device.

Nobody else will be able to see anything in your account. Everything else stays completely private.

How will my family know what to do? Show answer Hide answer

You can appoint a trusted person — like a family member or close friend — to be the main person who manages things after you're gone. We provide them with clear, simple instructions on what to do and what to pass on to others.

Each person you've shared items with will only see what you've given them access to — nothing more.

Can I leave a personal message for my family? Show answer Hide answer

Yes. When you add a contact, there is a space where you can write a personal message or special instructions just for them.

This might be something like: "The insurance documents are in the blue folder in my filing cabinet" — whatever would be most helpful for them to know.

Using This Website
How do I get started? Show answer Hide answer

Getting set up is straightforward and you can go at your own pace. Here is the order we suggest:

  • First, add the names and contact details of your trusted family members or friends.
  • Then add your important documents, accounts, and devices one at a time.
  • For each item, you can choose which person should have access to it.
  • There is no rush — you can update or add things any time.
How often do I need to update my information? Show answer Hide answer

We suggest checking your information once a year — for example, around the new year or your birthday. It is also a good idea to update things after any big changes in your life, such as:

  • Moving to a new home
  • Opening or closing a bank account
  • A change in your family situation
  • Updating a legal document like a will
Can I use this on my phone or tablet? Show answer Hide answer

Yes. This website works on phones, tablets, and computers. You do not need to download any separate app.

If you find the text too small, you can make it larger in your phone or browser settings. On most devices, you can also pinch the screen to zoom in.

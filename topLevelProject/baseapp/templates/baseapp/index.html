What you organize

Six categories. Every corner of your digital life.

Each category is built to answer a specific question your family will have — not just where to log in, but what to do next.

Trusted Contacts

Designate who handles what — executor, caregiver, attorney. Roles assigned, authority clear.

Digital Accounts

Banking, social, streaming, utilities — each with decommission instructions so nothing lingers.

Devices

Physical location, PINs, and 2FA access for every phone, computer, and tablet you own.

Estate Documents

Will, power of attorney, healthcare directive — tagged for exactly when they apply.

Important Documents

Insurance, property records, financial accounts — stored where your family can find them.

Funeral Planning Legacy

Final wishes, arrangements, and instructions — nothing left to guesswork or family disagreement.

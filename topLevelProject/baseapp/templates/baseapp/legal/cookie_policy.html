1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Digital Estate Plan ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website to improve user experience, analyze site performance, and maintain security.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your browser and remember certain information such as preferences, login status, or analytics data.

Cookies may be classified as:

Essential Cookies Necessary for website operation (e.g., security, login, session management).
Performance Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our website through anonymous analytics.
Functional Cookies Enable enhanced features and settings, like remembering user preferences.
Third-Party Cookies Set by other websites or services that appear on our site (for example, analytics or video embeds).
3. How We Use Cookies

Digital Estate Plan uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the secure functioning of our site.
  • To understand how visitors interact with our pages and improve usability.
  • To measure traffic and performance using privacy-focused analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Plausible.
  • To store consent preferences when you accept or reject cookies.
We do not use cookies to track you across other websites, nor do we use them for advertising or behavioral profiling.
4. Third-Party Tools and Integrations

Our website may include integrations or links to third-party platforms (e.g., Bitwarden API, file storage providers, or form tools). These services may set their own cookies or tracking scripts.

We have no control over those cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy or cookie policies of any linked third-party service you access through our website.
5. Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View, block, or delete existing cookies.
  • Set preferences for how cookies are handled.
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored.
Please note: Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the website function.

For guidance, visit  www.allaboutcookies.org  or your browser's help section.

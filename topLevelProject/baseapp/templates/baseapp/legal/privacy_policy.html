Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

At Digital Estate Plan ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our digital estate organization services.

By using our website or submitting any personal information, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver and improve our services. The types of information we may collect include:

Personal Information Name, email address, and phone number (if voluntarily provided).
Client Project Data Basic details you share while setting up or managing your digital estate records.
Uploaded Files Any files you choose to securely upload for your private record organization are encrypted and stored in a dedicated secure system.
Communication Data Messages, emails, or other correspondence you send us.
Technical Data Browser details, IP address, and site analytics to improve functionality.
We do not request or store sensitive data like full Social Security numbers, banking passwords, or government-issued identification unless directly necessary for secured digital estate planning support and authorized by you.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our digital estate record organization and support services.
  • Communicate regarding service requests, updates, or inquiries.
  • Maintain account and access security using the Bitwarden Password Manager API.
  • Improve website performance and user experience.
  • Fulfill legal, regulatory, or compliance obligations.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.
4. Secure File and Password Storage

We take data protection seriously and apply multiple layers of security to protect client information.

File Security Uploaded documents are stored using encrypted cloud storage that complies with industry best practices. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with access limited to authorized team members under strict confidentiality.
Password Security All password credentials are handled exclusively through the Bitwarden Password Manager API, which uses end-to-end encryption. We do not have access to your Bitwarden master password or plaintext credentials. Bitwarden's zero-knowledge model ensures data remains private to you at all times.
5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses limited analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Plausible) to understand visitor traffic and improve content. These tools collect only aggregated and anonymized data. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Information Sharing

We share data only under these limited conditions:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., when collaborating with approved family members or agents).
  • With trusted service providers essential to our operations, such as Bitwarden and encrypted storage platforms, under strict data protection agreements.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
We never share, publicize, or sell client data for marketing purposes.
7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw consent to data storage or processing at any time.

All requests may be made by contacting  privacy@digitalestateplan.com .

8. Data Retention

We retain personal and project data only as long as necessary to deliver services or meet compliance requirements. You may request full data deletion, including uploaded files, at any time.

9. External Links and Third-Party Integrations

Our site may contain links to third-party tools, platforms, or resources (for example, Bitwarden, encrypted cloud storage providers, or estate planning references). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational, technical, or legal changes. The "Last Updated" date above will always indicate the latest version. Your continued use of our website or services signifies acceptance of any updates.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

1. Introduction

At Novatern ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our digital estate organization services.

By using our website or submitting any personal information, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver and improve our services. The types of information we may collect include:

Personal Information Name, email address, username, and phone number (if voluntarily provided).
Estate Planning Data Digital account records, device information, contacts, documents, and other estate-related details you enter while using the service.
Encrypted Vault Credentials Passwords or PINs you choose to store in the Password Vault are encrypted with AES-256 (Fernet) before being saved. Login identifiers (username or email) associated with vault entries are stored in unencrypted form alongside the encrypted password token.
Uploaded Files Any files you upload as part of your estate records are stored on our servers. Access is restricted to your authenticated account and any recovery access you authorize.
Vault Access Logs Each time a vault password is revealed, an audit record is created containing the accessing user, timestamp, and IP address. These logs are retained for security and accountability purposes.
Communication Data Messages, emails, or other correspondence you send us.
Technical Data Browser details, IP address, and site analytics to improve functionality.
We do not request or store full Social Security numbers, full financial account numbers, routing numbers, or government-issued identification numbers. Passwords for financial institution accounts (banking, brokerage, cryptocurrency, and payment services) cannot be stored in the Password Vault and are explicitly blocked by the system.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our digital estate record organization services.
  • Manage your account, subscription tier, and access permissions.
  • Communicate regarding service requests, updates, or inquiries.
  • Maintain session security, audit trails, and account lockout protections.
  • Improve website performance and user experience.
  • Fulfill legal, regulatory, or compliance obligations.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.
4. Password Vault and Credential Storage

The Password Vault (available to Legacy subscription holders) allows you to store passwords and PINs for your digital accounts and devices. The following practices govern how vault data is handled:

Encryption All passwords are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet symmetric encryption) before being written to the database. Plaintext passwords are never persisted. Encryption keys are stored in server environment variables, not in the database.
On-Demand Decryption Only Passwords are decrypted only when you explicitly request a reveal within your authenticated session. Revealed passwords are automatically hidden after 15 seconds.
Financial Account Restriction The system blocks storage of passwords for financial institution accounts, including Online Banking, Neobank/Digital Bank, Brokerage/Investment, Cryptocurrency Exchange, Payment Processor, and Payment Wallet accounts. This restriction is enforced at both the interface and server level.
Audit Logging Every password reveal is logged with the user account, date and time, and IP address of the request. These logs cannot be deleted by users and are retained for security purposes.
User Responsibility and Risk Users are required to acknowledge inherent security risks before saving any vault entry. While we apply strong encryption, no online system is completely immune to breach. You accept responsibility for the credentials you choose to store.
5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses limited analytics tools to understand visitor traffic and improve content. These tools collect only aggregated and anonymized data. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

6. Information Sharing

We share data only under these limited conditions:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., when a family recovery request is approved by our team).
  • With trusted infrastructure providers (such as payment processors and hosting services) under strict data protection agreements. We use Stripe for payment processing — we do not store your payment card details on our servers.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
We never share, publicize, or sell client data for marketing purposes.
7. Subscription Tiers and Access

Access to features is governed by your subscription tier:

  • Free (no subscription): Full edit access up to per-category item limits (2 contacts, 1 account, 1 device, 1 estate document, 1 important document, 2 family notes). View access to all saved data.
  • Essentials: Full edit access while your subscription is active, up to higher per-category limits (3 contacts, 3 accounts, 3 devices, 3 estate documents, 5 important documents, 5 family notes).
  • Legacy: Full edit access with no item limits while your subscription is active, including the encrypted Password Vault and funeral planning features.
  • Lapsed subscription: View-only access at the Free Tier Level to all previously entered data . Edit access requires an active subscription.

Subscription status and tier limits are enforced at the server level on all data modification operations.

8. Family Recovery Access

Novatern includes a family recovery system that allows a designated contact to request access to your estate records in the event of death or incapacitation. Recovery requests require administrative review and are granted only upon verification. Time-limited access tokens are used to control the scope and duration of recovery access.

You control which contacts are designated as recipients of your estate records through the Contacts section of your dashboard.

  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw consent to data storage or processing at any time.

All requests may be made by contacting  privacy@novatern.com

We retain personal and project data only as long as necessary to deliver services or meet compliance requirements. Vault access logs are retained for security and accountability purposes. You may request full data deletion, including uploaded files, at any time. See our Data Retention Policy for specifics.

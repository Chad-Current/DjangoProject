Data Collection Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

At Digital Estate Plan ("we," "our," or "us"), your privacy and data security are our highest priorities. This Data Collection Policy explains what information we collect, how we collect it, and how it is used and protected in the course of delivering our digital estate organization services.

This policy applies to all information collected through our website, communications, and any secure client storage platforms we operate or integrate with.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information only as needed to provide, secure, and improve our services. The types of data we may collect include:

Personal Identification Data Name, email address, phone number, and general contact information voluntarily provided by you.
Account and Service Information Information you share or upload when using our digital estate organization tools, consultations, or secure file services.
Authentication Data Credentials stored and managed securely through the Bitwarden Password Manager API, never accessible in plain text.
Technical Data Browser type, IP address, device information, and website activity (collected via analytics) to enhance site performance and reliability.
Communication Data Correspondence or support requests sent to us by email, form, or chat.
We do not sell or rent any collected data to third parties.
3. How Information Is Collected

We collect data in several ways:

  • Directly from you when you contact us, create an account, request a consultation, or upload files.
  • Automatically through essential cookies, analytics tools, and server logs that monitor website performance.
  • Through integrations with trusted third-party services such as Bitwarden or encrypted cloud storage providers, strictly for secure functionality.
4. How We Use Collected Data

Your information may be used to:

  • Provide requested services, including file management and digital organization.
  • Facilitate communication and support.
  • Ensure secure authentication and password management using Bitwarden integration.
  • Improve our website, services, and security infrastructure.
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.
We do not use personal data for marketing or profiling purposes unless you expressly opt in.
5. Data Storage and Security

We use encrypted and access-controlled systems to keep your data safe.

Encryption All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using modern encryption standards such as AES-256 and TLS 1.3.
Password Protection Passwords and credentials are managed entirely through the Bitwarden API under its end-to-end zero-knowledge security model.
Access Control Only authorized personnel with legitimate service needs may access limited client data.
Secure Storage Uploaded files are hosted on trusted encrypted cloud services with redundant backups and activity monitoring.
6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations or meet legal and regulatory requirements. You may request permanent deletion of your personal information or uploaded data at any time by contacting  privacy@digitalestateplan.com .

7. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share limited data only:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • With service providers who support our operations (e.g., Bitwarden, encrypted storage vendors) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
We will never sell, lease, or trade confidential information with outside organizations.
8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Request a summary of data stored about you.

Contact  privacy@digitalestateplan.com  to exercise any of these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Collection Policy periodically to reflect updates in laws, technology, or business practices. All revisions will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

For questions about this Data Collection Policy or data protection practices, please contact:

5. Security and Data Protection

We take security seriously and use encrypted technologies to safeguard your information.

File Storage All uploaded documents are stored on our servers with access restricted to your authenticated account and any recovery access you have authorized.
Password Vault Encryption Passwords stored in the Password Vault are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet symmetric encryption) before being written to the database. Plaintext passwords are never persisted. Encryption keys are maintained in server environment variables. Passwords are decrypted only when you explicitly request a reveal within an authenticated session and are automatically hidden after 15 seconds. Every reveal is logged with the user, timestamp, and IP address.
Financial Account Restriction The system does not permit storage of passwords for financial institution accounts, including Online Banking, Neobank/Digital Bank, Brokerage/Investment, Cryptocurrency Exchange, Payment Processor, and Payment Wallet account types. This restriction is enforced at both the interface and server level and cannot be bypassed.
Session and Account Security Authenticated sessions expire after 1 hour of inactivity. Repeated failed login attempts will result in a temporary account lockout. Users may log in with either their username or email address.

How it works

A complete plan your family can actually use.

Most people think digital estate planning means writing down passwords. It means something much bigger — and we built the tool to do it right.

The real problem
"A list of passwords is the smallest thing your family will need. What they actually need is to know who's in charge, what to close, what to keep, and where everything is."

Without a plan, every one of these questions falls on grieving family members. Most of them never get answered.

  • 💳

    No decommission plan

    Subscriptions keep charging indefinitely because no one knows which services existed — or how to cancel them

  • 👤

    No role assignment

    Family members conflict over who has authority — or worse, no one steps up because no one was ever designated

  • 📄

    No document tagging

    Legal papers get found too late, misapplied, or not at all — because no one knew when each document was supposed to be used

  • 📱

    No device access

    Two-factor authentication codes are locked on a phone no one can unlock — making every other account inaccessible

What you organize

Six categories. Every corner of your digital life.

Each category is built to answer a specific question your family will have — not just where to log in, but what to do next.

Trusted Contacts

Designate who handles what — executor, caregiver, attorney. Roles assigned, authority clear.

Digital Accounts

Banking, social, streaming, utilities — each with decommission instructions so nothing lingers.

Devices

Physical location, PINs, and 2FA access for every phone, computer, and tablet you own.

Estate Documents

Will, power of attorney, healthcare directive — tagged for exactly when they apply.

Important Documents

Insurance, property records, financial accounts — stored where your family can find them.

Funeral Planning Legacy

Final wishes, arrangements, and instructions — nothing left to guesswork or family disagreement.

The process

Getting organized is simpler than you think.

Most people complete their plan in just a few hours — and feel the relief immediately.

Discover

Use our guided prompts to uncover every digital account you have — banking, utilities, social media, subscriptions, and more. You may be surprised how many you find.

Organize

Assign each account, device, and document to a trusted contact. Decide who gets access, what they can do, and when — so nothing is left for your family to figure out alone.

Rest Easy

Your plan is secure and ready. Built-in review reminders keep it current, and your family has a clear path forward whenever they need it.

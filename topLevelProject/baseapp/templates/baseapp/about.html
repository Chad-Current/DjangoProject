Security & trust

Your data is sensitive. We treat it that way.

Every decision we've made about how data is stored, accessed, and recovered starts with the question: what would we want if this were our family?

  • 🔐

    Encrypted at rest

    Stored credentials are encrypted using Fernet symmetric encryption before they ever touch our database. Your data is never stored in plaintext.

  • 🛡️

    Access controls you define

    You decide who sees what. Contacts have access only to what you explicitly delegate — nothing more, nothing less, and never without your instruction.

  • ✅

    Verified family recovery

    Family access requests go through a documented review process. Your information is never handed over without proper verification — protecting you and your family alike.

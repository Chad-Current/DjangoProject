1. INTRODUCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
At Novatern ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed
to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy
explains how we collect, store, and protect your information when you visit our
website or use our digital estate organization services.

By using our website or submitting any personal information, you agree to this
Privacy Policy.


2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We collect only the information necessary to deliver and improve our services.
The types of information we may collect include:

Personal Information
  Name, email address, username, and phone number (if voluntarily provided).

Estate Planning Data
  Digital account records, device information, contacts, documents, and other
  estate-related details you enter while using the service.

Encrypted Vault Credentials
  Passwords or PINs you choose to store in the Password Vault are encrypted
  with AES-256 (Fernet) before being saved. Login identifiers (username or
  email) associated with vault entries are stored in unencrypted form alongside
  the encrypted password token.

Uploaded Files
  Any files you upload as part of your estate records are stored on our
  servers. Access is restricted to your authenticated account.

Vault Access Logs
  Each time a vault password is revealed, an audit record is created containing
  the accessing user, timestamp, and IP address. These logs are retained for
  security and accountability purposes.

Communication Data
  Messages, emails, or other correspondence you send us.

Technical Data
  Browser details, IP address, and site analytics to improve functionality.

NOTE: We do not request or store full Social Security numbers, full financial
account numbers, routing numbers, or government-issued identification numbers.
Passwords for financial institution accounts (banking, brokerage,
cryptocurrency, and payment services) cannot be stored in the Password Vault
and are explicitly blocked by the system.

4. PASSWORD VAULT AND CREDENTIAL STORAGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Password Vault (available to Legacy subscription holders) allows you to
store passwords and PINs for your digital accounts and devices. The following
practices govern how vault data is handled:

Encryption
  All passwords are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet symmetric encryption)
  before being written to the database. Plaintext passwords are never
  persisted. Encryption keys are stored in server environment variables, not
  in the database.

On-Demand Decryption Only
  Passwords are decrypted only when you explicitly request a reveal within
  your authenticated session. Revealed passwords are automatically hidden
  after 15 seconds.

Financial Account Restriction
  The system blocks storage of passwords for financial institution accounts,
  including Online Banking, Neobank/Digital Bank, Brokerage/Investment,
  Cryptocurrency Exchange, Payment Processor, and Payment Wallet accounts.
  This restriction is enforced at both the interface and server level.

Audit Logging
  Every password reveal is logged with the user account, date and time, and
  IP address of the request. These logs cannot be deleted by users and are
  retained for security purposes.

User Responsibility and Risk
  Users are required to acknowledge inherent security risks before saving any
  vault entry. While we apply strong encryption, no online system is completely
  immune to breach. You accept responsibility for the credentials you choose
  to store.

7. SUBSCRIPTION TIERS AND ACCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Access to features is governed by your subscription tier:

  - Free (no subscription): Full edit access up to per-category item limits
    (2 contacts, 1 account, 1 device, 1 estate document, 1 important document,
    2 family notes). View access to all saved data.
  - Essentials: Full edit access while your subscription is active, up to
    higher per-category limits (3 contacts, 3 accounts, 3 devices, 3 estate
    documents, 5 important documents, 5 family notes).
  - Legacy: Full edit access with no item limits while your subscription is
    active, including the encrypted Password Vault and funeral planning
    features.
  - Lapsed subscription: View-only access at the Free Tier Level to all
    previously entered data. Edit access requires an active subscription.

Subscription status and tier limits are enforced at the server level on all
data modification operations.

DATA RETENTION POLICY
Novatern
Last Updated: April 13, 2026
================================================================================


1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This Data Retention Policy explains how Novatern ("we," "our," or "us")
retains, archives, and deletes personal and client information collected through
our website, services, and secure storage systems.

It applies to all digital records, uploaded files, support communications, and
limited technical data we handle in the course of providing digital estate
organization services.


2. PRINCIPLES OF RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We follow these core principles when retaining data:

  - Keep data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected or as
    required by law.
  - Minimize the amount of personal information stored and avoid indefinite
    retention.
  - Apply appropriate security controls to all retained data.
  - Delete or anonymize data once it is no longer needed.


3. DATA CATEGORIES AND RETENTION PERIODS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Unless a different period is required by law or agreed with you in writing, we
generally apply the following retention periods:

Account and Contact Information
  Name, email, phone, and basic profile details. Retained for up to 3 YEARS
  after the last meaningful interaction (login, service use, or direct
  contact), then deleted or anonymized.

Client Project and Service Records
  Notes related to your digital organization project, configuration details,
  and non-legal documentation. Retained for up to 5 YEARS after the completion
  of services, unless you request earlier deletion.

Uploaded Files and Documents
  Files stored with us for organizational purposes. Retained for the duration
  of the active service and up to 1 YEAR after service completion, unless you
  request immediate deletion or extended retention.

Support and Communication Records
  Email, messages, and service notes. Retained for up to 3 YEARS from the date
  of the last communication, primarily for service history and dispute
  resolution.

Technical and Analytics Data
  IP address, device type, usage logs, and analytics. Retained in identifiable
  form for up to 12 MONTHS, after which it may be aggregated or anonymized for
  long-term trend analysis.

Password Vault Entries
  Encrypted vault entries are retained for the duration of your active account.
  Upon account deletion, all vault entries and their encrypted content are
  permanently destroyed and cannot be recovered.

Vault Access Logs
  Audit logs recording each password reveal (user, timestamp, IP address) are
  retained for up to 3 YEARS from the date of the access event. These logs
  cannot be deleted by users and are retained for security accountability and
  potential legal purposes.

NOTE: If applicable law requires a longer retention period (for example, tax
or business records), we will retain the relevant records for the legally
required period.


4. ARCHIVING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
When data is no longer actively used but must be retained for legal, security,
or limited business reasons, we may move it into a secure archive.

  - Archived data is stored in encrypted environments with strict access
    controls.
  - Access to archived data is restricted to authorized personnel only and used
    solely for compliance, security, or documented business needs.


5. DELETION AND DISPOSAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Once a retention period expires and no legal or operational reason remains to
keep the data, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Electronic Records
  Permanently deleted from active systems and, where technically feasible,
  from backups according to our backup rotation schedule.

Anonymization
  In some cases, data may be anonymized so it can no longer be linked to an
  identifiable person, and may be retained for statistical or planning
  purposes.


6. LEGAL HOLDS AND EXCEPTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If we become aware of a legal claim, investigation, audit, or other legal
requirement relating to specific data, we may place a "legal hold" on that
data.

  - During a legal hold, affected data will not be deleted -- even if its
    normal retention period has expired -- until the hold is lifted.
  - Once the legal hold ends, normal retention and deletion rules will resume.


7. YOUR RIGHTS AND REQUESTS FOR DELETION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at
any time, subject to legal and contractual limitations.

We will honor verified requests to delete personal and uploaded data unless we
are required to keep some records for legal, regulatory, or security reasons.

To submit a request, contact us at privacy@novatern.com with sufficient
information to verify your identity and identify the records in question.


8. RESPONSIBILITIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We maintain internal roles and responsibilities to support proper retention and
disposal:

Management / Owners
  Define retention rules and ensure this policy aligns with legal requirements
  and business needs.

Technical / IT Support
  Implement secure storage, archiving, and deletion procedures in line with
  this policy.

All Staff and Contractors
  Handle data according to this policy and avoid creating or retaining
  unnecessary copies of personal information.


9. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We may update this Data Retention Policy periodically to reflect changes in
laws, technology, or our services. Any updates will be posted on our website
with an updated "Last Updated" date, and continued use of our services
signifies acceptance of the revised policy.

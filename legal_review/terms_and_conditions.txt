7. SECURITY AND DATA PROTECTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We take security seriously and use encrypted technologies to safeguard your
information.

File Storage
  All uploaded documents are stored on our servers with access restricted to
  your authenticated account.

Password Vault Encryption
  Passwords stored in the Password Vault are encrypted using AES-256 (Fernet
  symmetric encryption) before being written to the database. Plaintext
  passwords are never persisted. Encryption keys are maintained in server
  environment variables. Passwords are decrypted only when you explicitly
  request a reveal within an authenticated session and are automatically hidden
  after 15 seconds. Every reveal is logged with the user, timestamp, and IP
  address.

Financial Account Restriction
  The system does not permit storage of passwords for financial institution
  accounts, including Online Banking, Neobank/Digital Bank,
  Brokerage/Investment, Cryptocurrency Exchange, Payment Processor, and Payment
  Wallet account types. This restriction is enforced at both the interface and
  server level and cannot be bypassed.

Session and Account Security
  Authenticated sessions expire after 1 hour of inactivity. Repeated failed
  login attempts will result in a temporary account lockout. Users may log in
  with either their username or email address.

User Responsibility
  You are responsible for securing your own account credentials and for all
  information you choose to store in the platform. Before saving any vault

  storing credentials online.

NOTE: While we apply strict security protocols, no system is completely immune
to breach. By using the Services, you acknowledge this inherent risk and agree

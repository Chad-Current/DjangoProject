2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
They help websites recognize your browser and remember certain information such
as preferences, login status, or analytics data.

Cookies may be classified as:

Essential Cookies
  Necessary for website operation (e.g., security, login, session management).

Performance Cookies
  Help us understand how visitors use our website through anonymous analytics.

Functional Cookies
  Enable enhanced features and settings, like remembering user preferences.

Third-Party Cookies
  Set by other websites or services that appear on our site (for example,
  analytics or video embeds).


3. HOW WE USE COOKIES

NOTE: We do not use cookies to track you across other websites, nor do we use

5. MANAGING AND DISABLING COOKIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You can control or delete cookies through your web browser settings. Most
browsers allow you to:

  - View, block, or delete existing cookies.
  - Set preferences for how cookies are handled.
  - Receive notifications before cookies are stored.

NOTE: Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the website function.

For guidance, visit www.allaboutcookies.org or your browser's help section.

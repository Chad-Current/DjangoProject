CONTACT ROLE ASSIGNMENTS — FIELD DESCRIPTIONS
Novatern
================================================================================
The following are the help text descriptions for each assignable role on a
Contact record. Each role is a boolean flag that can be independently assigned
to any contact.
================================================================================


Emergency Contact
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Contact to notify and provide access during medical emergencies or sudden
incapacity.


Digital Executor
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Responsible for managing digital assets (accounts, passwords, online services)
after death.


Caregiver
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Primary person providing or coordinating long-term care needs and health
decisions.


Legal Executor
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Named executor/personal representative to administer overall estate and will.


Trustee
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Manages trusts, distributions, and trust assets according to trust terms.


Financial Agent
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Authorized under financial power of attorney to handle money/bills if
incapacitated.


Healthcare Proxy
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Makes medical decisions if you're unable to communicate (healthcare power of
attorney).


Guardian for Dependents
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Named legal guardian for minor children or dependent adults.


Pet Caregiver
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Responsible for pet care, veterinary decisions, and related expenses.


Memorial Contact
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Handles funeral, memorial service, and burial/cremation preferences.


Legacy Contact
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Designated for platform-specific legacy access (e.g., Apple, Google,
Facebook).


Professional Advisor
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Attorney, accountant, or advisor to consult for legal/financial guidance.


Notification Only
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Receives notifications and basic info but has no decision-making authority or
tasks.


Knowledge Contact
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Delegated specific knowledge/info (e.g., account locations) but no action
required.
